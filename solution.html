Simple expression evaluator

Click here for story and requirements.

Evaluates expressions containing numbers and variables, using operators +, -, *, /, and ^ for exponentiaton.
For grouping, uses parentheses: ( and ). Unary minus is either first character, or first character after a (.

Can handle two types of expressions:

  • Assignments, such as:
    price = 45.00 * items - discount

  • Arithmetic expressions, such as:
    2 * 3.1415 * (radius + 0.5)

Type your expressions below, and click [Calculate] or press [Enter].
This triggers expression evaluation, and displays the calculated value (or syntax error).
